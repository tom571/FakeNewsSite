What is Fake News?

Definition

False stories that appear to be news, spread on the internet or using other media, usually created to influence political views or as a joke.

How to spot it

Consider the Source

Investigate more

Click away from the story to investigate the site, its mission and its contact info.


Check the Author

Do a quick search on the author. Find out if they are credible, and if they are real.

Read Beyond

The full picture

Headlines can be outrageous in an effort to get clicks. Make sure you read the whole story, to get a better picture.


Look for Supporting Sources

Click on those links. Determine if the information given actually supports the story

Check The Date

Recently posted

Reposting old news stories doesn't mean they're relevant to current events


Is it a Joke?

If it is too outlandish, it might be satire. Make sure to research the site and author to be sure.
